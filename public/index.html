Mission

Governments around the world have repeatedly shown a callous disregard for individual privacy. Warrantless searches of personal information abound. Haven gives back the power of high-grade secrecy to the masses.

Your files are safe with us.

Technology

Haven works by ensuring that your files never pass over any network connection. Before any communication occurs between your computer and our servers, Haven encrypts your files using the proven Blowfish cipher. Even if someone were to listen to every last packet of information exchanged, they would be no closer to having your data.
